About Joe

Before hitting the road recently, I was working for an awesome agency in San Francisco, Sequence, and before that at Viscira.

As a freelancer, I am excited to take on most web-based projects, and I outline the typical services below. Since I am traveling, most projects will likely need to be remote, but I work well with teams and independently. Also, if you need something unique that is not listed, please do not hesitate to reach out to me.
