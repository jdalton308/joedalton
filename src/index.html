About

After spending four years in San Francisco, I moved to Colorado in 2016 to find a more balanced life. I'm currently living in Denver and enjoying both the rock climbing and tech culture of the area.

I have been doing front-end development for the last 5 years, working for a few different agencies and product companies. I most enjoy collaborating with designers on great user experiences, and get excited about brand-focused motion, animation, and highly interactive interfaces. Having built dozens of stand-alone, single-page apps, I consider myself primarily a JavaScript developer, versed in most of the modern frameworks, principles, and best practices, but I have a deep appreciation for design and UX principles.

For contract or freelance work, I am excited to take on most web-based projects. The work can be as routine as converting your designs into a static site, or collaborating with your team to plan, design, and develop a sophisticated app. Shoot me an email, and we can discuss how I can help.
